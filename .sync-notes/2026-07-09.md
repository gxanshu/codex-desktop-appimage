### The load-bearing CI job

For an AppImage fork the decisive job is **Build App Against Upstream DMG**
(`upstream-build-app.yml`): it downloads the real upstream DMG and runs
`make build-app`, proving the merged patch set applies and the app builds
end-to-end. That is what actually gates this fork.
